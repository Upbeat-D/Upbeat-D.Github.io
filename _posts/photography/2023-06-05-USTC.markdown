From April to June this year, I visited the University of Science and Technology of China. It has a strong science and engineering atmosphere, which is completely different from Northwest University. I often go to 1948 Cafe to drink iced Americano. The bitter taste can calm my mood. When I'm not in the lab, I hang out on campus or take their courses. Unfortunately, during these two months, I did not have the opportunity to visit other places in Hefei.
